A Provocative Series of Embroidered Fashion Photos That Offer the Artist’s Alternative Vision of BeautyJose Romussi, a Chilean artist based out of Berlin, Germany, offers an alternative perspective of beauty with his provocative series “Anti-Serie“. In the series, Romussi embroidered gorgeously chaotic patterns onto fashion photos, creating fascinating results.

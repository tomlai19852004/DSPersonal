Artist Illustrates Her Own Cat In the Varying Styles of Famous Comics and CartoonsWebcomic artist and animation student Miyuli of “Hearts for Sale” and “Lost Nightmare“, illustrated her own cat in the varying styles of different comics and cartoons. Some of the more notable sketches are of Miyuli’s cat as Cat in the Hat, Garfield, Pusheen and of course, the ever-hungry Simon’s Cat.via DesignTaxiOriginally published at laughingsquid.com on November 18, 2015.
